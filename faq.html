Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting or building an overland truck

Before you rent +

What are the rental requirements?

You'll need a valid driver's license (held for minimum 2 years), minimum age 25. You will need to show the drivers license during pickup.

How do I book the truck?

You can book through our Goboony page. For longer rentals (3+ weeks), please contact us directly so we can discuss options and pricing.

What's included in the rental?

The rental includes the fully equipped Ford Ranger with dual rooftop tents, basic camping equipment, kitchen supplies, mattress covers, and recovery gear. Insurance and breakdown coverage are also included. Sleeping bags and pillows are not included.

Is there a minimum rental period?

We have a minimum rental period of 2 days.

Can I take the truck outside the Netherlands?

Yes! The truck is insured within Europe. Other international trips are possible - just let us know your destination when booking so we can ensure you have the right documentation.

How about toll?

Toll charges are your responsibility. We'll let you know if there's a valid toll vignette on the car at the time of rental.

Can I bring pets?

Maybe! Please let us know in advance so we can provide appropriate cleaning afterwards. An additional cleaning fee may apply.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we ask for a 1200 euro security deposit in addition to the 1250 own deductible. The security deposit is released within 48hrs after the truck is inspected at return.

Can I drive the truck to another country and leave it there?

No, the truck is always to be returned to the pickup location. All made costs for recovering the car will be charged to the renter including the fee of 1000 euro per day of travel.

Pickup details +

Where do I pick up the truck?

Pickup is typically from our location in the Netherlands (Voorburg area). We'll provide exact address and directions when you confirm your booking.

What do I need to bring for pickup?

Please bring your valid driver's license and payment method for the security deposit. We'll provide a detailed handover and walkthrough of all equipment.

What about insurance?

Insurance and breakdown coverage are included in your rental through Goboony. You'll be responsible for the deductible amount in case of damage. We'll explain all coverage details during pickup.

Do you offer delivery?

No, pickup is from our location in Voorburg.

How long does the pickup process take?

Plan for about 30-45 minutes. We'll walk you through all the truck's features, camping equipment, show you how to setup the tent and awning, and answer any questions you have. We want to make sure you feel confident before you hit the road!

During your rental +

What fuel type does the truck use?

The Ford Ranger runs on diesel. The truck is fuel-efficient for its size and capability, averaging around 10L/100km depending on driving conditions.

What are the dimensions of the truck?

The truck is 5m40 long, 2m2 wide (including the mirrors) and is 2m45 high.

Help, it says 'add ad-blue' on the screen

No panic! You can find ad-blue at almost all gas stations across Europe. Just fill up the tank using the little blue fill-up option behind the fuel flap.

What if I need help with either the tent, the awning or the equipment?

No panic! First time using the equipment may be new to you. Please contact us and we will talk you through the steps or help you get on your way again.

What if I have mechanical issues during my trip?

Breakdown coverage (Europe only) is included in your rental. Please call ANWB roadside support on +3188 269 2100. Don't forget to inform us as well!

Can I extend my rental?

If the truck isn't booked after your rental period, extensions are usually possible. Contact us as soon as you know you'd like to extend, and we'll check availability and arrange the extension.

What happens if I damage the truck?

Insurance covers most damage, but you'll be responsible for the deductible amount (1250 euro). We inspect the truck together before and after your rental to document its condition. Always report any incidents immediately.

Is camping equipment included?

Yes! We provide camping chairs, table, cooking equipment, and basic kitchen supplies. You just need to bring sleeping bags, pillows, food, personal items, and your sense of adventure!

What should I do at the end of my rental?

Return the truck with a full tank of diesel, clean interior, and all equipment accounted for. We'll do a final walk-around together, check for any damage, and release your security deposit if everything is in order.

Build a truck +

Do you build custom overland trucks?

Yes! We offer custom overland truck builds. Whether you want a simple rooftop tent setup or a full expedition vehicle, we can help design and build your dream adventure truck.

What's the typical timeline for a build?

Build timelines vary depending on complexity. A basic setup (2nd battery, charger and solar panels) typically takes 2-4 weeks. More complex builds with custom fabrication can take 3-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and will keep you informed about delayes due to delivery times of parts.

Can you work with my existing vehicle?

Absolutely! We can outfit your existing truck or 4x4 with overland equipment. We'll assess your vehicle and recommend the best setup for your needs and budget.

How much does a custom build cost?

Costs vary widely based on your requirements. A basic rooftop tent setup starts around €3,000-5,000, while full expedition builds can range from €10,000-30,000+. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

Do you provide maintenance after the build?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance and support for all our builds. We can help with equipment servicing, upgrades, and repairs to keep your overland setup in top condition.
