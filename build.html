Key Products & Upgrades

Here are the main products that made this build possible

Sheepie Yuna 140 Rooftop Tents

Premium hardshell rooftop tents that provide comfortable sleeping for up to 4 people. Weighing 55kg each, these tents open in seconds and offer weather protection with excellent ventilation.

  • All-season comfort
  • Integrated LED lighting
  • Premium mattress included
